About Cherish

Biography

Cherish Banhotra is a 20-year-old singer-songwriter from Delhi, India, known for her western music and fusion of various genres.


She made her professional debut in 2022 and has since released several songs, including collaborative tracks, and has garnered a significant number of streams on social media platforms. She has also been featured in musical projects with international artists and performed at various events.

Music Career

Cherish draws inspiration from a wide range of artists across pop, indie, folk, and classical music. Her arrangements often pair acoustic guitar or piano with subtle electronic textures, allowing the voice and story to remain front and center.
